Screen Recording

  1. Welcome to our survey. For this survey, you will be asked to share your camera, and your screen. Please share your browser screen on which you are taking the survey and your camera and microphone
  2. Click Start Recording
  3. Your browser will ask for permission to access your screen. Select which application/sreen you would like to record.
  4. Once you are finished, click Stop Recording, and you will be prompted to download the recording
